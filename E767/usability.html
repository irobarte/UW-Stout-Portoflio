Usability Test

QUESTION COMMENTS
Is the navigation and table of contents clearly labeled?
Is the branching appropriate for the content presented? How would you improve navigation in the module?
Does the branching work?
Is the progress tracker at the bottom of the screen useful?
Is the information presented in a logical, organized way when viewed in sequential or non-sequential order? If not, how could the organization be improved?
Is the narration clear and understandable?
Are the images provided appropriate and helpful? Do the images need further explanations?
Is the information provided enough to answer the knowledge check questions? If not, which sections are missing information?
Is the theme (colors, font, etc.) appropriate? How would you alter, adjust or improve the theme?
From the Topic Selection screen (#2), select a topic and answer the following questions:
• Was the path taken effective? If not, what confused you?
• Did you have any problems completing the task?
Do the slides provide clear explanations of the steps? Would someone that is not listening to the audio be able to understand the training or the slides?
Were you able to navigate the module only using the keyboard?
What did you like best?
What did you like least?
What other suggestions or comments do you have?
